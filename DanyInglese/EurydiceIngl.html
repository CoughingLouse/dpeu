The Ensemble Eurydice was founded in France in July 1989 by an international group of musicians. It recreates the original ‘Divertimento Concerts’ with repertoire that goes back to the music of the courts and the baroque. It also reaches to the present with music of our time, notably, it included compositions especially for the Ensemble Eurydice (Daniela Pisano flute, Jacques Tys oboe, Vincent Malgrange cello and Denise Wolfcarius cembalo).

 

A central place is however kept for the vast chamber music repertoire of the XVII an XVIII centuries (Bach, Vivaldi, Quantz, Telemann...).

 

The Ensemble Eurydice is able to perform in variable sized and offers programmes with diverse formations, starting with a duo an up to chamber orchestra with soloists, singer and conductor.

 

The Ensemble Eurydice organized the summer courses « Eurydice » in Levico Terme (Italy).
